Interactive Vietnamese Alphabet (letters and tones) + Quiz
==========================================================

Instructions:
------
1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).
2. Click on any letter to play its pronunciation.
3. "Quiz Mode" plays a random sound; click the correct letter to score points.


How to use this locally:
------------------------
Download the contents of the audio folder and img folder plus index.html. Place them in your chosen directory on your computer.

Structure:
----------
vietnamese_alphabet_project/
│
├── index.html
├── audio/
│   ├── a.mp3
│   ├── á.mp3
│   ├── à.mp3
│   └── etc.
└── img/
    └── alphabet.png
